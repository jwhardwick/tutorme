Welcome to TutorMe

Australia's hub for university tutoring.

TutorMe is a place for students and tutors to connect. It's free to sign up for, and can help you get matched with someone who can help.

If you're a student, you can find a high achiever who's an expert in the course you're taking. It's easy to connect with them and book a session to get some 1 on 1 teaching and help further you understanding.

If you're a tutor, this is a great way to earn some extra money doing something fun - teaching! All with a flexible arrangement with no minimum required working hours. You set the price, and the students will come. Just let us know when you're available, and what subjects you offer.
